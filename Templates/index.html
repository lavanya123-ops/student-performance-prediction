{# Methodology #}

Working

Understand How Student Performance Prediction Works

Methodology
1. Define Goals

We start by identifying the purpose — predicting student scores and highlighting key performance factors.

2. Collect Data

Student-related data such as demographics, study hours, parental education, and test preparation details are gathered.

3. Clean & Validate

The collected data is cleaned, standardized, and validated to ensure consistency and accuracy for model training.

4. Data Processing

Key features such as math, reading, and writing scores are processed to prepare for accurate prediction.

5. Model Training

Machine learning algorithms are trained on historical student data to recognize patterns in performance.

6. Prediction

The trained model predicts future student performance in different subjects with high accuracy.

7. Report Generation

The results are presented in an easy-to-understand format, highlighting strengths and weaknesses.

8. Feedback & Improvement

Based on the prediction, actionable feedback and study recommendations are provided for better outcomes.
